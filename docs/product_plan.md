# Product Plan

Moon Jar Stories is a premium Korean folktale library for children ages 3-8, built as native iOS/iPadOS and Android/tablet apps over one shared content engine.

## Positioning

- Korean folktale living picture books with English-first demo narration
- Optional Korean and bilingual text/audio support
- Gentle narration and bedtime mode
- Parent-safe subscription
- No ads, no child accounts, no third-party analytics in the child experience

## Launch Catalog

- 24 catalog entries
- 5 free books and 19 premium books with complete shared-content payloads
- Premium books stay locked by entitlement until purchase/subscription access is granted
- Monthly new books after launch

## Platform Strategy

iPad should be the flagship reading experience. Android should ship as a first-class native product because Korean families include a large Android audience, especially in Korea.

The apps should not fork story content. Native teams own rendering, animation, audio, billing, accessibility, and offline behavior. The shared content pipeline owns text, translation, narration scripts, vocabulary, prompts, asset references, and animation metadata.
