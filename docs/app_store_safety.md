# App Store Safety

## Child Experience

- No ads
- No child accounts
- No public profiles
- No open chat
- No third-party analytics SDKs in the child reader
- No behavioral ad tracking

## Parent Gate

Purchases, subscription screens, restore flows, external links, settings that affect privacy, and account actions must sit behind a parent gate.

The prototype uses a simple arithmetic gate. Production should rotate age-appropriate parent-only challenges and comply with each store's family policy.

## Content Safety

Traditional folktales often include death, cruelty, frightening punishment, or outdated gender dynamics. Moon Jar adaptations should:

- Preserve cultural identity and story shape.
- Remove graphic harm.
- Reframe fear as suspense or humor for ages 3-8.
- Use consent-forward rewrites where needed.
- Avoid shame-based morals.
- Let characters repair mistakes.

## Privacy

Grandparent voice mode should require explicit parent action. Recordings should remain local by default. Cloud sync, if added, needs clear consent, deletion controls, and family-scoped access.
