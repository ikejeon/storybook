# Product Sense

Moon Jar Stories is a premium Korean folktale storybook product for families, especially U.S. families who want an English-first experience with meaningful Korean language and cultural layers.

## Product Principles

- English-first for U.S. demos, with Korean always available and respected.
- Korean folktales should feel warm, memorable, rhythmic, and read-aloud friendly.
- Tension is allowed when child-safe; do not flatten every story into a summary.
- Asset status must stay honest: draft is draft, reviewed is reviewed, final is final.
- The reader should feel like a premium living picture book, not a generic media app.
- No ads, no child tracking, no child accounts, and no live AI in the child app.

## Experience Targets

- iPad/tablet: premium two-page spread, warm hanji pages, deep indigo frame, center gutter, subtle controls, refined page turn.
- Phone: single-page portrait reader, large fit-safe illustration, parchment text panel, clean controls, no accidental cropping.
- Narration: professional, calm, warm, and trustworthy. macOS synthetic voices are draft only.
- Story art: child-safe Korean watercolor/gouache/ink direction, character-consistent, no text in images, no accidental subject cropping. Runtime draft art must have manifest dimensions, visual-review state, and native fit-safe/layered presentation before an all-catalog demo score is claimed.

## Current Product Truth

The repo is a serious prototype and production-operational foundation, not a shippable product. Repo-local visual-system and Backend/CMS readiness are now close to all-catalog demo complete, with deterministic validators, local backend state, CMS export, review queue, and release-readiness summaries. Production blockers remain: final reviewed art, final narration, final sounds, real payments/receipt verification, deployed backend/CMS UI, store packaging, legal/privacy review, and broader QA.
